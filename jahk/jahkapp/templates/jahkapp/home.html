JA Innovation Camp is a full-day

JA experiential program that builds STEM and soft skills (creativity, entrepreneurial mindset, teamwork and problem solving) of students, and inspire them to apply their learnings to develop a solution for a problem that they identify in their communities through team exercise, prototyping and presentation. Students will be motivated to take initiatives to find innovative solutions to real-life problems and contribute to the community.

JA Personal Spending

The program helps students develop a responsible attitude towards personal spending. Business volunteers share with the students their financial management experiences to help students understand the concepts of income, saving, expense, donation, budget and differentiate between “needs” and “wants”.
